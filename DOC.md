# 🌙 The Dream Compiler Spec
## *Where Your Words Become Worlds*

---

> **Version:** 1.0 — "The Imagination Engine"  
> **For:** Dreamers ages 8 to infinity  
> **How it works:** You say it. We build it. Block by block.

## 1. ✨ The Grammar of Dreams

Every dream starts with a **wish**. When you type or say a dream, the Dream Compiler listens for special **Dream Words** and turns them into voxel magic. Here is the secret recipe:

```
DREAM = [Size] + [Color] + [Thing] + [Modifiers] + [Connectors] + [More Things]
```

### The Dream Recipe:
| Ingredient | What It Does | Example |
|------------|--------------|---------|
| **WISH WORD** | Starts the dream (optional but fun!) | "I want", "Give me", "Build" |
| **SIZE** | How big the thing is | "tiny", "massive" |
| **COLOR** | What color the blocks are | "crystal", "golden" |
| **THING** | The core structure | "tower", "castle", "garden" |
| **MODIFIER** | Special effects and behaviors | "glowing", "floating" |
| **CONNECTOR** | How things stick together | "with", "on top", "around", "and", "inside" |

### 🗺️ Phrase → Structure Map

| Phrase | What It Becomes |
|--------|-----------------|
| "tower" | A tall stack of blocks, thinner than it is tall, with a pointy or flat top |
| "castle" | A big blocky building with walls, towers on corners, and a courtyard inside |
| "garden" | A patch of green blocks with little colorful flower voxels scattered around |
| "moat" | A ring of water-blue blocks surrounding something, usually 2 blocks deep |
| "bridge" | A thin arch of blocks connecting two places over empty space or water |
| "house" | A small cube with a triangle roof, a door, and maybe a window |
| "tree" | A brown trunk with a fluffy ball or cone of green blocks on top |
| "pond" | A flat circle or square of water blocks, sometimes with lily pads |
| "path" | A strip of gravel-colored blocks leading from one thing to another |
| "dungeon" | An underground room made of dark stone blocks, maybe with a torch |
| "cloud" | A fluffy cluster of white blocks floating in the sky |
| "cave" | A hollow blob of stone blocks with a dark entrance |
| "statue" | A stack of blocks shaped like a person or creature |
| "bridge" | An arch or flat strip connecting two places |
| "ladder" | A vertical strip of blocks with rungs for climbing |

## 3. 📏 Size Adjectives: How Big Is Your Dream?

Size words tell the Dream Compiler how many blocks to use. Every thing has a **default size**, and the adjective scales it up or down!

| Size Word | Multiplier | Example |
|-----------|------------|---------|
| **Tiny** | ×0.5 | A tiny castle is the size of a dollhouse |
| **Small** | ×0.75 | A small tower is perfect for a fairy |
| *(no size word)* | ×1.0 | The normal, everyday size |
| **Big** | ×1.5 | A big house has extra rooms |
| **Huge** | ×2.0 | A huge tree is taller than a real house |
| **Massive** | ×3.0 | A massive mountain fills your whole screen! |

### Special Size Rules:
- You can only use **one** size word per thing. If you say "tiny small house," the compiler picks the FIRST one (tiny) and giggles.
- Sizes stack with connectors! `"a huge castle with a tiny garden"` makes a giant castle with a adorable miniature garden beside it.

## 5. 💫 Modifiers: The Special Effects

Modifiers are like superpowers for your structures! They change HOW the blocks behave, not just how they look.

| Modifier | What Happens | Example |
|----------|--------------|---------|
| **Glowing** | The blocks emit soft light, like lanterns! | `"a glowing cave"` is never dark inside |
| **Floating** | The structure hovers in the air with no support | `"a floating island"` drifts in the sky |
| **Underwater** | The thing is built beneath the water surface | `"an underwater garden"` has fish swimming through it |
| **Hidden** | The thing is camouflaged or buried | `"a hidden door"` looks like a normal wall until you get close |
| **Burning** | Fire particles dance on top (but doesn't destroy blocks!) | `"a burning torch"` flickers with orange light |
| **Frozen** | Blocks have ice crystals and shimmer cold | `"a frozen pond"` has a glassy surface you can slide on |
| **Haunted** | Ghostly particles float around; spooky sounds! | `"a haunted house"` has creaky doors and mist |
| **Musical** | The blocks play soft notes when you walk near | `"a musical garden"` makes bells and chimes as you explore |
| **Bouncy** | Walking on it makes you jump extra high! | `"a bouncy castle"` is the most fun castle ever |

### Modifier Stacking:
You CAN stack modifiers!  
`"a glowing floating castle"` = a castle that hovers in the sky AND lights up the night!

## 6. 🧩 Composites: Sticking Dreams Together

A **composite** is when two or more dreams become one big dream! The Dream Compiler uses **connector words** to snap structures together like LEGO bricks.

### Connector Words (The Glue!)

| Connector | How It Builds | Diagram |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| **with** | Adds something as a buddy | `A + B` side by side |
| **and** | Adds another thing | `A + B + C` all friends |
| **on top** | Stacks vertically | `B` sits on `A`'s roof |
| **inside** | Makes a container | `B` is within `A`'s walls |
| **around** | Wraps a ring | `B` encircles `A` |
| **under** | Goes below | `B` is beneath `A` |
| **beside** | Next to | `B` stands at `A`'s side |
| **connected by** | Bridge/link | `A` and `B` joined by `C` |

### Composite Examples:

| Dream | What Gets Built |
|-------|-----------------|
| `tower + garden` | A tower with a grassy roof-garden on top |
| `castle + moat + bridge` | A castle surrounded by water, with a bridge leading to the front door |
| `house + tree + path` | A cozy house next to a tree, with a little walkway between them |
| `dungeon + ladder` | An underground room with a ladder leading down into it |
| `cloud + castle + rainbow bridge` | A sky palace on a fluffy island, connected to another cloud by a rainbow arch |
| `pond + tree + garden` | A peaceful park with a pond in the middle, trees for shade, and flowers everywhere |

### The Order Rule:
The **main thing** always comes first. `"a garden with a tower"` puts the garden on the ground and the tower rises from it. `"a tower with a garden"` puts the garden ON TOP of the tower!

## 7. 🏗️ The Master Builder: How Dreams Compose

Let's build something AMAZING, step by step, to show how the Dream Compiler thinks.

### Your Dream:
> **`"I want a tall golden tower with a glowing garden on top and a moat around it"`**

### Step-by-Step Compilation:

```
🧠 STEP 1: Find the MAIN THING
   Word: "tower"
   → Start building a tower!

🧠 STEP 2: Apply Adjectives
   "tall"  → Size: BIG (×1.5 height boost!)
   "golden" → Color: shiny gold blocks
   → Build a tall, golden tower! (30 blocks high, gleaming!)

🧠 STEP 3: Look for Connectors
   Found: "with"
   → Something is coming to join the tower...

🧠 STEP 4: Find the Next Thing
   "glowing garden" 
   Modifier: "glowing" → light-emitting blocks
   Thing: "garden" → grass + flowers
   → Build a glowing garden...

🧠 STEP 5: Apply Position
   "on top" → Place the garden on the tower's roof!
   → The tower now has a radiant, flower-filled garden at its peak!

🧠 STEP 6: Find Next Connector
   Found: "and"
   → Another thing is coming!

🧠 STEP 7: Find Final Thing
   "moat around it"
   Thing: "moat" → water ring
   Position: "around" → encircle the tower
   → Dig a water ring surrounding the whole tower!

🎉 FINAL RESULT:
   A gleaming golden tower stretching to the sky, crowned with a 
   luminous garden of glowing flowers, all protected by a calm ring 
   of water at its base. MAGICAL!
```

### Another Complex Example:

> **`"Build a hidden underwater cave with a glowing crystal inside and a tiny bridge over it"`**

**Result:** A secret cave beneath the ocean waves. Inside, a pulsing crystal gives off soft light. Above the cave's entrance (underwater but with an air pocket), a miniature bridge arches over a trench. Only the bravest divers can find it!

## 8. 🌩️ Error Handling: When Dreams Get Cloudy

Sometimes a dream doesn't make sense—or the Dream Compiler gets confused. Don't worry! Instead of breaking, it tries its BEST and explains what happened.

### Error Types (And What Happens!)

#### 🔮 The "Huh?" Dream — Unclear Words
**You say:** `"I want a flibbertigibbet tower"`  
**What happens:** The compiler doesn't know "flibbertigibbet." It builds a NORMAL tower and pops up a friendly message:
> *"I didn't know what 'flibbertigibbet' means, so I built you a regular tower instead! Try words like 'golden,' 'crystal,' 'tall,' or 'tiny'! 🌟"*

#### 🎭 The Conflicting Dream — Opposites
**You say:** `"I want a tiny massive house"`  
**What happens:** The compiler picks the FIRST size word and winks:
> *"You said 'tiny' AND 'massive'—that's a funny puzzle! I picked 'tiny' because it came first. Want to try again with just one size? 🏠"*

#### 🌊 The Impossible Dream — Physics Says No
**You say:** `"I want a floating underground castle"`  
**What happens:** The compiler can't float something AND bury it. It picks the COOLER option (floating) and says:
> *"Floating AND underground is a tricky mix! I made it FLOATING so you can show it off to the sky birds. If you wanted buried, try 'hidden' instead! 🏰☁️"*

#### 🗝️ The Missing Dream — Not Enough Words
**You say:** `"I want a"`  
**What happens:** The compiler gently prompts:
> *"Your dream feels incomplete! What do you want? A tower? A garden? A dragon? (Okay, I can't do dragons YET... but tell me more!) 🐉"*

#### 🧱 The Too-Big Dream — Size Overflow
**You say:** `"I want a massive massive massive castle"`  
**What happens:** The compiler laughs and caps the size:
> *"Whoa, that's SO MASSIVE it wouldn't fit in the world! I built you a MASSIVE castle (the biggest I can do). It's still ENORMOUS! 🏰✨"*

### The Golden Rule of Errors:
**Never destroy, never crash, always build SOMETHING!**  
Every dream deserves a try. If the compiler is really stuck, it builds a **Mystery Block** (a rainbow-colored question mark cube) and asks you to tell it more.
